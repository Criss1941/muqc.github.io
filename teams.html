Teams

MUQC currently has two teams: the Manchester Manticores who are more competitive and the Manchester Minotaurs who are more casual. Having these two teams allows us to cater to a wider range of people.

Manticores

The Manticores are lead by Matthew Fenton, with assistance from Vice Captain Tua Karling. Fenton has played Quidditch since October 2015 primarily as a Chaser/Keeper, becoming captain in Feburary 2017.
